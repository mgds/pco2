Welcome to paleo-co2.org!

This site provides access to published paleo-CO2 data that have been assembled and curated by an international group of proxy experts. An expert-vetted data compilation is currently being assessed and will be made available in the future, along with explanatory animations and videos. Content and functionality of this site are updated frequently, so please check back soon and follow us on Twitter for periodic update announcements.
