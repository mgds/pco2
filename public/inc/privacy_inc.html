Privacy Policy
When You Visit Our Website

When you sign up to use OceanVideoLab, you may be asked to provide us with the following information: your first name, last name, and email address. By signing-in using your Google account, you are authorizing OceanVideoLab to collect, store, and use, in accordance with this Privacy Policy, any and all information that you have authorized Google to provide to OceanVideoLab. Such information may include your first and last name, unique identifier, e-mail address and picture.

Web Statistics

As part of standard internet practice, we collect standard web log information such as IP address, date/time of access, type of browser and operating system when you visit our site. We use IP addresses to analyze trends, track usage of system features and tools, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use for reporting and sponsorship purposes. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information and we do not expose any personally identifiable information from our logs.

Use of Cookies

We use cookies on our site to aid with user authentication. You can reject cookies by following the directions provided by your browser vendor or by your Internet provider's "help" file. If you reject cookies, you may still visit the Site, but will not be able to log into your account. We do not maintain a historical database of cookies, nor do we use any other tracking method to profile users.

Email Sent to our Help Desk

When you send an electronic mail message to our Help Desk, you may be sending personal information. In these cases, we retain the information only to respond to your request. We do not share this information with any other organization or entity.

Video Contributions

If you use our webform to contribute videos to our OVL or if you contact us directly for video submission, we capture the contact information you provide and use it to respond to your comments, requests, and inquiries, to request additional information regarding your submission if needed, and to notify you when your videos are published. This information will be maintained for future communications, but to the maximum extent of the law, is not provided to any other organization or entity.

Video Tags

If you use our citizen science or science user interface to tag videos, we capture your basic user information. We will not expose any personally identifiable information unless specifically requested at registration and then only to provide you with proper credit for your contributions to scientific research.

Notification and Correspondence

We may notify Contributors via email when their videos have been submitted or when citizen scientists have a tag approved. We may contact you to send you newsletters and service updates. If you wish to opt out of notifications, please contact us.
