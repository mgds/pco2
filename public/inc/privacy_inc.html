Privacy Policy

Web Statistics

As part of standard internet practice, we collect standard web log information such as IP address, date/time of access, type of browser and operating system when you visit our site. We use IP addresses to analyze trends, track usage of system features and tools, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use for reporting and sponsorship purposes. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information and we do not expose any personally identifiable information from our logs.

Sharing

We will share aggregated demographic information with our sponsors. This is not linked to any personal information that can identify any individual person. We only share this information so that we can secure funding to continue providing this site free of charge.

Links

This Website contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we do not claim any responsibility for the privacy practices of such other sites. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this website.

Email Sent to our Help Desk

When you send an electronic mail message to our Help Desk, you may be sending personal information. In these cases, we retain the information only to respond to your request. We do not share this information with any other organization or entity.
