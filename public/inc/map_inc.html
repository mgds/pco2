  2. Paleo-CO2 Map View

Explore sampling sites and related data and studies in this interactive map. Click on symbols to bring up detailed information. The legend is accessible from the top right of the interface, and symbols are colored based on proxy. The filter symbol in the lower left allows you to select proxies and time windows of interest. Use the arrow at the bottom of the interface to expand and view the tabular data used in the map. All positions used in this interface are based on modern latitude/longitude, and we are working on additional representations and maps of these data using paleo-coordinates
