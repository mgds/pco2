Welcome to the Phanerozoic CO2 Proxy Integration Project (CO2PIP)!

This is the ongoing phase of the paleo-CO2 project and aims to (1) expand the paleo-CO2 proxy data repository to the entire Phanerozoic, (2) modernize existing CO2 proxy records, (3) develop forward proxy system models for the paleosol, leaf-based, phytoplankton, and boron isotope proxies, and (4) statistically integrate proxy models and data to generate a robust, quantitative reconstruction of Phanerozoic atmospheric CO2. In addition, CO2PIP develops (5) a set of 2-D and 3-D tools for users to experience regional CO2/climate connections, and (6) paleo-CO2 teaching modules tailored to undergraduate and graduate students.

Instructions

In the graph, click and drag or select an epoch in the plot to zoom. Zoom out using the magnifying glass icon. Click on the proxy names in the legend to toggle their visibility. Use the download data button to download values and their associated references.

Data Sources

    • 10.5281/zenodo.10078932 (vetted paleo-CO2 product estimates)

Opportunities for you to get involved!

This project is just starting and if you are a paleo-CO2 expert we would like to engage with you through a series of workshops, sharing of samples and data for the purpose of documenting and modernizing published records, and funding opportunities for relevant analyses and/or research visits to strengthen the knowledge base and scientific community. Please email if you are interested in attending one of the introductory planning workshops (see calendar for dates), and please check back soon and follow us on LinkedIn for periodic update announcements.
