Welcome to paleo-co2.org!

This site will provide access to a compilation of proxy CO2 data that is being assembled and curated by an international group of proxy experts. Our goals are to facilitate access to these data, provide details of each proxy, explanatory animations and videos, and interactive plots that allow users to explore these important data. Please check back soon for updates!
